JOIN THE F.O.R.C.E

FRESNO'S ORGANIZATION RESPECTING CLEAN ENVIRONMENTS

Project-Waste F.o.r.c.e is a local initiative to help put an end to pollution in the central valley. Here are 10 easy ways residents can contribute to a more eco-friendly Fresno.

  1. Scale back vehicle use. Carpool use a bike or walk.
  2. Cancel paper statements and opt for e-receipts.
  3. Turn off electronics you are not using.
  4. Recycle especially bottles, cans and glass etc.
  5. Buy from locally owned stores.
  6. Decrease water consumption. Time showers and sink use.
  7. Air dry clothes.
  8. Plant a tree.
  9. Stop smoking outside. Smoke in a garage or indoors.
  10. Spread the word!
